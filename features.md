# INRCLIQ — Product Features

INRCLIQ is a creator-led social platform where fans discover content, follow creators, listen to audio, shop collections, request personalized experiences, and message creators — with age-appropriate onboarding and parental controls for younger users.

---

## Accounts & Access

- **Sign up** — Fans create an account with name, date of birth, and location; the product routes them as an adult or minor based on age.
- **Email verification** — New accounts confirm ownership of their email before continuing.
- **Login** — Returning users sign in with email + password or a one-time email code.
- **Forgot login** — Users who forget their password can request a login code by email.
- **Logout & sessions** — Authenticated sessions gate the feed and related surfaces.

---

## Onboarding

- **Guided setup** — After signup or login, users are walked through remaining steps until their profile is ready for the feed.
- **Password setup** — Adults (and approved minors) can set a password for future logins.
- **Handle selection** — Users choose a unique `@handle` for their identity on the platform.
- **Interest selection** — Users pick topics they care about (music, sports, gaming, and more) to personalize their experience.
- **Welcome completion** — Finishing onboarding confirms the profile is ready and brings users into the live feed.

---

## Safety & Parental Controls (Minors)

- **Minor account path** — Users under the age threshold must complete guardian approval before using the product.
- **Parent / guardian invite** — Minors invite a parent or guardian by email and wait for a decision, with status, expiry, and resend.
- **Guardian approval journey** — Guardians open an invite link to review and approve or decline the child’s account.
- **Guardian identity & context** — Approval includes guardian account details, identity steps, and where the child lives relative to the guardian.
- **Protection levels** — Guardians choose Strict, Standard, or Relaxed protection for how the child’s experience is managed.
- **Child notifications** — Children are notified when a guardian approves or declines their account.

---

## Home Feed

- **Creator timeline** — Fans browse posts from creators (text, tags, images, and audio).
- **Category filters** — Users refine the feed by interest or discovery categories.
- **Engagement actions** — Posts support like, comment, share, hide, and follow creator actions.
- **Members-only content** — Subscriber-locked media is presented distinctly for members-only posts.
- **Top creators & suggestions** — Rails highlight creators and suggested people to follow.
- **Stories & snaps** — Short-form story and snap surfaces appear in the feed experience.
- **Responsive navigation** — Desktop and mobile layouts support browsing, messaging, and audio.

---

## Audio

- **Audio hub** — A dedicated audio destination for podcasts, music, and audiobooks.
- **Browse & discover** — Charts, “for you,” genres, live drops, and continue-listening style sections help fans find audio.
- **Playback** — Mini, dock, and fullscreen players support listening from the feed and the audio hub.

---

## Creator Profiles

- **Public creator pages** — Fans view cover, bio, verification, popular posts, and a creator’s feed.
- **Follow creators** — Users can follow creators from posts and profiles.
- **Subscribe & notifications** — Fans subscribe to creators and choose notification levels (all, personalized, or none).
- **Profile navigation** — From a profile, fans can open the creator’s shop, special requests, and related content.

---

## Creator Shop (Collections)

- **Creator storefronts** — Creators offer physical and digital products through a collection shop.
- **Product browsing** — Fans filter listings, open product detail, and review pricing, ratings, and offers.
- **Shopping bag** — Add to bag, adjust quantities and variants, and manage a per-creator cart.
- **Checkout** — Fans complete checkout with shipping details, promo codes, and payment options (including cash on delivery for physical items where shown).
- **Order confirmation** — A success experience confirms the purchase flow.

---

## Special Requests

- **Bookable experiences** — Fans explore creator offerings such as shout-outs, coaching, and appearances.
- **Service discovery** — Categories, galleries, example work, pricing ranges, and reviews help fans choose an experience.
- **Personalized booking** — Fans configure delivery method, content type, recipient, timing, duration, and location for appearances.
- **Location search** — Appearance bookings support place search so fans can specify where an event should happen.

---

## Messaging

- **Inbox** — Fans open conversations with creators and support-style contacts.
- **Thread search** — Users find conversations in their inbox.
- **Send & read messages** — Fans send messages, view history, and see unread indicators in navigation.

---

## Communications

- **Transactional email** — The product can send verification, login codes, parent invites, approval/decline notices, and onboarding-complete emails.
- **In-app email previews** — Prototype inbox views help demonstrate verification and login-code emails during demos.

---

## Operations & Admin

- **User directory** — Operators can list and search registered accounts.
- **Account removal** — Operators can permanently delete a user.
- **Platform reset** — A development reset clears accounts and related data for a clean demo slate.

---

## Product summary

| Area | Business value |
|------|----------------|
| Accounts & onboarding | Trusted signup with verified identity and a clear path into the product |
| Parental controls | Safer underage access with guardian consent and protection choices |
| Feed & audio | Discovery and engagement around creator content and listening |
| Profiles & subscribe | Fan–creator relationships with follow and notification control |
| Shop & special requests | Monetization via merch/digital goods and personalized creator services |
| Messaging | Direct fan–creator communication |
| Admin tools | Prototype operations for managing demo accounts |
